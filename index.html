Aesop Equilibrium: Find your balance
2024, Year 2 School Project
with groupmates Kuang Ranran, Hur Inhaeng and Levana Halim
Brand Campaign Identity Design

Brief:
Working under the allocated brand, create an integrated brand campaign that engages with the target audience from multiple platforms, channels and touchpoints.


Work-life balance is not a new topic but it has always been relevant as life is getting faster and busier day by day. Inspired by the art of rock balancing, Equilibrium by Aesop is a campaign that aims to become an occasion for everyone to focus more on the inside as well their beloved and revisit the concept of “work-life balance" in order to help them find their own “balance" in life.

Aligned with the theme of work-life balance, we also introduce a new collection of Gift Kits, inspired by James Clear's philosophy: The 4 Burners Theory, each kit is meticulously curated to honor the essential aspects of life: family, friends, work, and health. With a deep understanding of the importance of nurturing these areas, Aesop has carefully selected most cherished formulations to cater specifically to your loved ones.

The City Remembers

130mm x 230mm, 56 pages
Kettle stitch binding

2024, Year 2 School Project
Publication Design

Brief:
Design a publication centred around a poem of your choice, selected from the book ‘A History of Amnesia’ by Singaporean Poet Alfian Sa’at.


This publication illustrates my personal feelings and understanding of the poem The City Remembers (from the book A History of Amnesia) by Alfian Sa'at. In this poem, from different object’s perspectives (the street lamp, the train,...), the poet explores the interaction between people, people with their surroundings, to reflect on their loneliness, lack of interaction, hesitation, and uncertainty.

Singapore Archifest 2024: STRUCTURALISM
2023, Year 2 School Project
with groupmate Hur Inhaeng
Festival Identity Design

Brief:
Create a visual identity system for the promotion of Singapore Archifest 2024


Singapore Archifest is an annual public festival celebrating architecture.

Singapore Archifest 2024: STRUCTURALISM, integrates the structural aesthetic values of architecture by combining architectural forms and urban planning for a cosmopolitan society. This informs our concept which accounts for the rich diversity of cultures and traditions, that constitute Singaporean society.

Our typeface and design system incorporates diverse architectural forms which reflect the visible structures that bring our society together, as the event emphasizes the harmony of different cultures and traditions that shape Singapore.

LASALLE DCE Reader
128mm x 178mm, 76 pages
Kettle stitch binding
2023, Year 1 School Project Publication Design
Publication Design

Brief:
Design a cover (front & back) and lay out the provided content across a minimum of 52 pages. Use only 1 grid system, 2 font families and one text color.



LASALLE DCE reader consists of five different articles spanning various fields of art and design. This first-year project aims to challenge students with design fundamentals: grid systems, hierarchy, and the task of connecting the five articles together cohesively within a book. It also encourages students to unleash their creativity in layout design despite the strict guidelines outlined in the brief.
